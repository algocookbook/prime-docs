‚úÖ Essential Risk Management Rules

  • Start with paper trading for at least 30-60 days
  • Never risk more than 1-2% of capital per trade
  • Always use stop-loss orders
  • Diversify across uncorrelated strategies

  • Monitor positions daily
  • Have an emergency exit plan
  • Keep detailed records for taxes
